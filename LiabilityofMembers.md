# 9. Liability of Members

a. No officer or member shall be personally liable for any bills or obligations of BEIC, past or present, except for the contribution of their own dues and assessments.
 
b. No officer or member of the BEIC shall disburse any assets, property, or resources belonging to BEIC without authorization of the President, ED/AM, and/or a simple majority vote of BoD members.  A majority vote may establish new maximum allowable expenses for routine purchases and unforeseen events, and other expenses.  The ED/AM is authorized the disbursement of funds in the for items and amounts as described in Section 7.  The ED/AM will maintain records for all expenses incurred by the organization.  If an individual incurs an expense to BEIC he/she must submit a voucher to the ED/AM for approval.
